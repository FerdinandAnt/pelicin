# Border

`border` sets the style and thickness of en element's border.

<Image path="/res/web/css/box-model.png" caption="Illustration of CSS Box model" />

## Basic Concepts

### Border Positions

You can set border(s) on the four sides of an element: top, right, bottom, left.

You can also specify border positions based on the direction of writing (these would be based on `writing-mode`, `direction`, and `text-orientation` values):

- <dfn>Inline direction</dfn> &mdash; is the direction of writing (e.g. left-to-right, top-to-bottom)

- <dfn>Block direction</dfn> &mdash; is the direction perpendicular to inline position (i.e. the direction
  of how you add new lines to your paragraph)
